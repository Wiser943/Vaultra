Our Mission

Why We Built VAULTRA

Nigeria has one of the fastest-growing creator economies in Africa. Nigerian creators on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube produce content that reaches millions globally — yet the monetisation tools available to them pay in Naira, leaving them exposed to currency devaluation and locked out of the global Euro economy.

VAULTRA exists to fix that gap. We built a platform where any Nigerian creator — beginner or experienced, small audience or large — can earn €2 per hour per stream, across 6 simultaneous VaultGain channels, and withdraw directly to any Nigerian bank account within 24–48 hours.

Our single activation model (₦14,000 one-time Glam Fee, no subscriptions) means the platform is accessible to everyone. We believe the barrier to earning in strong currency should be talent and time — not expensive subscription gates.

Our Journey

The VAULTRA Story

How we got here — and where we're going

Early 2026

The Problem Identified

Our founding team — Nigerian creators and digital marketers based in Lagos — noticed that every major creator monetisation tool available to Nigerians paid in local currency or required international payment methods most creators didn't have. We started mapping out a solution.

Mid 2026

Platform Built & Tested

The first version of VAULTRA's creator dashboard was built and piloted with a small group of creators across Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. The VaultGain structure — 6 simultaneous earning streams — was refined based on their feedback. Withdrawal infrastructure integrated with Moniepoint and major Nigerian banks.

Late 2026

Public Launch

VAULTRA opened to all Nigerian creators with the full 6-stream VaultGain model, one-time ₦14,000 activation, and the Glam Link Bonus referral programme. The platform grew through creator-to-creator referrals on TikTok, Instagram, and Telegram.

2026 — Present

Growing & Improving

We continue to expand support channels, add new content niches, and improve withdrawal speeds. GlamDarkMode (off-peak earnings) and GlamScript2Cash (per-script payments for writers) were added based on creator requests. More streams are in development.

Transparency Statement

Is VAULTRA Legitimate?

We know you've seen platforms come and go. Here's what makes VAULTRA different — and what we want you to know before you join.

What VAULTRA is

  • A legitimate Nigerian creator monetisation platform founded in Lagos in 2026, operated by a Nigerian team.
  • A one-time payment model — your ₦14,000 Glam Fee activates your account permanently. There are no monthly renewals, no hidden upgrade charges.
  • A real payment system — withdrawals go to Nigerian bank accounts via Moniepoint and major banks within 24–48 hours of request.
  • Fully transparent earning structure — €2 per hour per stream, €12/hour total across 6 streams. No hidden multipliers or conditions.
  • A referral programme with real limits — the Glam Link Bonus (₦12,000 per referral) is real but depends on your referred creators also activating their accounts. We don't promise passive income without effort.

What VAULTRA is not

  • Not a get-rich-quick scheme — earning €12/hour requires active task completion. The income is real, but so is the work involved.
  • Not a pyramid scheme — earnings from VaultGain streams are based on your personal task output, not solely on recruitment. The referral bonus is supplemental.
  • Not a subscription trap — you pay ₦14,000 once. If you decide VAULTRA isn't for you, there are no recurring charges.
