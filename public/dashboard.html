Quick Answers

How much does VAULTRA pay per hour?

VAULTRA Nigeria pays a total of €12 per hour in VaultGains. This is made up of €2/hour from 6 hourly channels (Vault Lifestyle, Vault Realtime, Vault FaceTime, Vault Works, Vault DarkMode, Vault Lingua) plus Vault Script2Cash which pays €2 per script submitted.

How long does verification take?

Manual verification takes 1–24 hours after you send your payment proof to our Telegram admin at t.me/VAULTRA_SUPPORT Send your receipt immediately after paying for fastest processing.

What is the Glam Link Bonus?

The Glam Link Bonus is a ₦12,000 (€6) instant cashback credited to your account immediately after you pay the ₦14,000 Glam Fee. You also receive a ₦10,000 Glam Reward on top.

How fast are withdrawals processed?

VAULTRA processes withdrawals within 24 hours to local Nigerian bank accounts and within 48 hours to domiciliary (DOM) accounts. The minimum withdrawal is €5.
