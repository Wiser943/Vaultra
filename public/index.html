What is VAULTRA and How Do Nigerian Creators Earn Euros?

The problem: Nigerian content creators produce world-class content but earn in weak local currency, missing out on the global Euro economy.

The answer: VAULTRA is a Nigerian creator monetisation platform that pays verified creators in Euros — specifically €12 per hour — through 6 simultaneous task streams, activated with a one-time ₦14,000 (€7) Glam Fee.

  • Vault Lifestyle — €2/hour from lifestyle content curation tasks
  • Vault Realtime — €2/hour from real-time live content engagement

  • Vault Script2Cash — €2 per approved branded content script written

The Glam Fee is ₦14,000 (€7) — paid once, never again. Vault Script2Cash pays creators €2 per completed and approved branded content script.
